About Us

Aageno sekuwaGhar

Aageno Sekuwa Ghar, is a Resturent located in Gathaghar, Bhaktapur, Nepal. It is a barbecue restaurant that specializes in traditional Nepali grilled dishes, particularly sekuwa, which is a popular street food in Nepal. The restaurant is known for its authentic flavors and cozy ambiance, making it a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike to enjoy delicious grilled meals.
